## Estimates

Regarding the **estimates** that you did for practical tasks...

### How was your estimate accuracy usually?

My estimate accuracy was generally improving over time, but it varied depending on the complexity of the tasks. For simpler tasks, I tended to be more accurate, while more complex tasks often took longer than I initially estimated.

### How did your estimate accuracy improve or change during the course of the subject?

As the subject progressed, my estimate accuracy improved. I became better at breaking down tasks into smaller components, which helped me make more accurate time estimates. I also learned to account for unexpected challenges and debugging time.

### What did you learn from doing these estimates?

I learned that estimation is a valuable skill in software development. It helped me manage my time better and set realistic expectations. I also learned the importance of reflecting on my estimates and actual time spent to continually improve my estimation skills.
